Join the numbers and get to the 2048 tile while answering popup questions!

Keep going Try again

How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Once in a while the game will ask you a JavaScript question. Answer the multiple choice correctly - and move where you wanted. Answer incorrectly - the move will be cancelled and new tiles will appear. Questions might have zero, one, several or all correct answers
